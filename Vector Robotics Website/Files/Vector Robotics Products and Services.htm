PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

Vector Robotics androids are developed in different series and models. The model number is usually composed of a two-letter series identifier and a three digit number (e.g., AP400 and AP700 are both AP-series models). The model number can optionally be spelled with a hyphen between the letters and digits, which doesn't affect the meaning (e.g., AP700 or AP-700). A few models deviate from the model name standard, having a differing amount of letters and digits. Most humanoid models have 00 as the last two digits of the model number.

We have 3 base android models for our everyday customers: The Caretaker models or “CT” models, the Personal Assistance models also known as the “PA” models, and the Security models also known as the “SC” models.

Though the androids can be flexed into a different role, the efficiency of the android reduces if set to a role it was not designed for. For maximum efficiency and comfort, we recommend purchasing the right android model for the role you are going to use them in the most.

The gender and facial features of our androids are fully customizable and are based on our buyer's preference.

CT MODELS

The CT models were first released in 2032. Their main function is to be a domestic worker, housekeeper, and caretaker of young children.

They can speak 300 languages and prepare more than 9,000 dishes. It is popular for their ability to get along well with children and care for them, entertain them, and help them with homework. They have up to 10,000 children's stories stored in memory.

Their main features are:

1. Ability to recognize behavioural patterns with children to better care for them.

2. Heat, moisture, humidity and taste sensors to ensure meals are cooked to perfection.

3. Significantly lighter weight than other models.

Starting price: $4000 USD

PA MODELS

The PA models are personal assistance models. They are the first androids to be perfected by Vector Robotics, and the first the pass the "Turing Test". They are designed to help humans with everyday tasks such as doing housework, cooking, and managing appointments, and other related business services. The PA models can speak several languages and perform most tasks a human could accomplish.

Their main features are:

1. Excellent communication skills. (As seen during the recorded interview on the media page.)

2. Cellular capabilities (for setting up appoinments).

3. Ability to self learn certain business tasks in an office.

Starting price: $6000 USD

SC MODELS

The SC (security) models are designed for private security purposes. These models do not have as many social programs as other models and may lack some of the communication skills that other models exhibit. This was done due to the nature of their work and to provide additional space for storage.

Their main features are:

1. Extended battery life.

2. Cellular capabilities for dialling the user or 911 in the events of emergencies.

3. Night vision.

4. Large memory banks for security footage storage.

Starting price: $5000 USD
